Our Mission

Rockingham Bicycle & Fabrication was founded in a backyard workshop in 2002 in Stokesville, Virginia, near the North River. At RB&F, we design and build high-end carbon fiber mountain and gravel bikes in small quantities compared to the big bicycle corporations. Every RB&F bike is built with meticulous detail, using high-end components and the best materials. Our design and fabrication personnel team have over 85 combined years in the aerospace, motorsport racing, and bicycle industries. RB&F bikes have won one Enduro World Series, two US Big Mountain Enduro Championships and two Red Bull Rampages. Our racing résumé also includes a solid list of top-ten podium finishes across both the United States and Canada.
