About Us

Our Story

Rockingham Bicycle & Fabrication was founded in a backyard workshop in 2002 in Stokesville, Virginia, near the North River. We are a small business, employing less than 50 people and are privately held, though employees may opt in and invest, making it an employee-owned company.

At RB&F, we design and build high-end carbon fiber mountain and gravel bikes in small quantities compared to the big bicycle corporations. Our current corporate headquarters is based in Harrisonburg, Virginia and we have just opened a new manufacturing facility in Rockingham County, just north of the city, increasing our production output.

Though RB&F is considered a "boutique" bike builder, our intention is to expand our product line within the next year. We have established a small, but dedicated dealer network in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast region states as well as California, Colorado, Washington, and Canada.

Our Products

Our standard models are carbon fiber mountain and gravel bicycles, built to order through a dealer or online retailer and then shipped to the retailer or directly to consumers. We currently have a small model range, but are developing two new bicycles to complement our current lineup. Every bike RB&F manufactures is built with meticulous detail, using high-endcomponents and the best materials. Our testing grounds are the chunky, technical trails around the North Mountain area as well as Massanutten, Snowshoe, and Bryce bike parks to ensure our bikes meet our expectations.

What sets us apart from other small bike manufacturers is that we are a true custom shop. Customers can select their frame size/configuration and RB&F will build a bicycle according to the supplied personal measurements, riding style and specifications. At RB&F, we personally build each bike, from hand-layering the carbon fiber frame, to building custom wheel sets, in combination with using only the best component groups selected by the customer.

Manufacturing

The RB&F design and fabrication team have over 85 combined years in the aerospace, motorsport racing, and bicycle industries. All of their frames are designed and fabricated in-house in Virginia, using state-of-the art CAD and manufacturing equipment, along with personal expertise and detailed testing.

Our Success

RB&F's riders have won one Enduro World Series, two US Big Mountain Enduro Championships, and two Red Bull Rampages. Their racing résumé also includes a solid list of top-ten podium finishes in the US and Canada. Currently, RB&F sponsors a team of three cyclists that tour the world circuit.
